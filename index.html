How long our coils last ?

Our coil can be last long for a month with proper use (average 2-4 weeks).
Factors that affected to coil lifetime: level of sweetness, quality of e-juice, level of VG, amount of cotton.

How to use our coil package as an atomizer stand ?

There's a hole on the bottom of our package (510 size), so you can flip the bottom up and use as an atomizer stand !
